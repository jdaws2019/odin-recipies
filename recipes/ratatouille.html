Ratatouille

Description

Ratatouille is a healthy dish using eggplant, squash, onions, tomatoes and herbs and spices.

Ingredients:

  • Tomatoes
  • Eggplant
  • Zuccini
  • Squash
  • Onions
  • Olive Oil
  • Spices

Steps:

  1. Peel and dice the eggplant
  2. Place in roasting dish with olive oil
  3. Dice tomatoes and onions and add to pot.
  4. Meld ingredients, place in oven for 45 minutes
