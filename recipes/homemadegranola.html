Ratatouille

Description

Granola is a convienient snack, but high in sugar so enjoy sparingly!

Ingredients:

  • Rolled Oats
  • Maple Syrup
  • Kosher Salt
  • Vegtable Oil

Steps:

  1. Combine syrup, salt and oil
  2. Stir into oats
  3. Spread on a cookie sheet
  4. Bake at 225F for 30 minutes
